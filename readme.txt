A WordPress plugin that brings your generated content into WordPress Posts.

== Description ==

NexMind is a powerful and innovative plugin that brings high-quality generated content to your WordPress site. Using advanced artificial intelligence technology from platfrom.nexmind.ai, the plugin creates unique articles and blog posts with just a few clicks. With NexMind, you no longer have to spend hours researching and writing content, or pay writers to do it for you. Simply choose your topic, adjust the parameters, and let NexWriter do the rest. Your blog will be updated with fresh, engaging content, giving your audience a reason to keep coming back. Try NexMind and see the difference that generated content can make for your site instantly.

== Installation ==

1. Upload 'NexMind' plugin files to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory, or install the plugin through the WordPress plugins Directory.
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' screen in WordPress.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= How do I use this plugin? =

1. Set up your account at platfrom.nexmind.ai.
2. Install 'NexMind' to your WordPress.
3. Insert the "User ID" and "Token ID" in the plugin "Users" page from the designed folder you created at platfrom.nexmind.ai.
4. Once the "User ID" and "Token ID" saved, you will be able to publish your generated contents to WordPress.
